The Trick

Multiply both sides by μ(x) = e^∫P dx. The left side becomes d/dx[μ·y] by the product rule. Then integrate both sides to get μ·y = ∫μQ dx, hence y = (∫μQ dx)/μ.

Solution Family
y = (1/μ)(∫μQ dx + C)
Changing C shifts the family vertically in the μ-scaled frame. The particular solution uses the initial condition y(x₀)=y₀ to pin C.
